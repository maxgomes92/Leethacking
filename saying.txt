Cattlexodus: 34:29
When Moohses came down from Muck Sinai with the two tablets of the corporate law in his hands, he was not aware that his face was radiant because he had spoken with the board. They had decided to claim ownership of the tablets and charge a philistine fee of 25% of the retail price for every reproduced unit. Moohses put a veil over his beast face, grabbed his bovine battle axe and went ballistic in the board room. People saw this and rejoiced, and so the first struggle for a copyright reform was launched.
